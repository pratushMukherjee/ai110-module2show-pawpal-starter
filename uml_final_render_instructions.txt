The final UML diagram is saved as `uml_final.mmd` (Mermaid markup).

To produce a PNG from this file, you can use a Mermaid renderer such as the `mmdc` (mermaid.cli) tool:

1. Install mermaid.cli (requires Node.js):
   npm install -g @mermaid-js/mermaid-cli

2. Render PNG:
   mmdc -i uml_final.mmd -o uml_final.png

Or paste the contents into an online Mermaid live editor and export a PNG/SVG.
